# Shopee Taiwan: Market Intelligence & Data Analysis Framework

## 1. Executive Summary
Taiwan is one of the most active and competitive e-commerce markets in the region. Shopee Taiwan reflects strong mobile commerce behavior, frequent promotion cycles, high consumer sensitivity to pricing, and intense competition across major categories. For brands, agencies, and data service providers, structured data analysis is essential to navigate this market and identify commercial opportunities.

---

## 2. Strategic Objectives
The core objectives of this data analysis framework are:
* **Identify Opportunities:** Discover high-growth product categories and subcategories.
* **Competitor Benchmarking:** Track leading stores, brands, and top-performing sellers.
* **Pricing Intelligence:** Analyze pricing patterns, discount behavior, and promotional trends.
* **Demand Evaluation:** Use sales, ratings, and reviews to evaluate customer demand and sentiment.
* **Support Business Decisions:** Provide data-backed insights for market entry, product selection, and data service sales.

---

## 3. Recommended Data Scope
A robust Shopee Taiwan analysis system should monitor the following dimensions:

| Data Dimension | Key Metrics | Commercial Value |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Product Data** | Title, category, brand, attributes, images | Keyword optimization & market positioning. |
| **Price Data** | Listed price, discounted price, vouchers | Competitive pricing & price elasticity analysis. |
| **Sales Data** | Estimated sales volume, sold count, trends | Market share tracking & revenue forecasting. |
| **Store Data** | Store name, ratings, followers, response rate | Seller reliability & brand authority benchmarking. |
| **Review Data** | Rating scores, review text, media attachments | Identifying product gaps & quality issues. |
| **Promotion Data** | Flash sales, bundles, free shipping signals | Mapping promotional strategies & peak demand. |

## 5. The 4-Layer Analysis Framework

### 5.1 Category Opportunity Analysis
* **Focus:** Identifying where demand exists and where competition is fragmented.
* **Key Metrics:** Monthly estimated sales, average selling price, and top seller market share.
* **Goal:** Find niches with strong demand but low brand concentration.

### 5.2 Competitor Intelligence Analysis
* **Focus:** Decoding the strategies of top stores and brands.
* **Key Metrics:** Store-level product count, review growth, and promotion frequency.
* **Goal:** Understand if leaders rely on price, branding, or high-volume promotions to stay on top.

### 5.3 Product and SKU Analysis
* **Focus:** Identifying high-performing product patterns.
* **Key Metrics:** SKU-level sales velocity and price-to-sales relationship.
* **Goal:** Determine which specific features and price ranges resonate most with Taiwanese consumers.

### 5.4 Consumer Feedback Analysis
* **Focus:** Turning raw review data into business insights.
* **Key Metrics:** Sentiment analysis of positive vs. negative feedback.
* **Goal:** Identify common complaints (e.g., delivery speed, packaging) to create a superior value proposition.
