# 🇲🇾 Shopee Malaysia: Strategic Data Acquisition & E-commerce Scraping

## 1. Executive Summary
Malaysia is a highly diverse and tech-savvy e-commerce market, serving as a critical hub for cross-border trade in Southeast Asia. Shopee Malaysia (MY) is characterized by a strong mix of local and international sellers, with high consumer engagement in specialized categories like Halal-certified goods and tech gadgets. Our **Web Scraping** infrastructure provides the granular data needed to navigate Malaysia's competitive multi-cultural consumer base.

Our **Data Extraction** framework for Shopee MY is optimized to handle:
* **Cross-Border vs. Local Monitoring:** Distinguishing between "Overseas" and "Local" sellers to analyze shipping competitiveness.
